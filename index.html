About the Author

Dione Rocha de Sousa (1987) is Brazilian and, since 2010, has dedicated his life entirely to missionary work. For over 12 years he has served in Indonesia, lovingly and committedly serving the Christian faith. Passionate about the country, he built his family with his Indonesian wife and is now the father of two children. Currently, along with his family, the author remains active in mission work, focusing on: * Discipleship * Contextual evangelism * Social action programs for children.
